PROJECT NAME
Country of Origin

OPPORTUNITY
Forum is a company that assembles products; therefore, it receives different items from all over the world. They must determine the Country of Origin of each of their finished goods depending on the regulations. They faced a situation where they could not determine the COO of their items.

SOLUTION
We developed a software that extracted data from their Bill of Materials (BOM). With this system the COO of each individual item is registered and when a final product is assembled, they have the information to determine the COO based on percentage of cost of items from a specific country, essential characters, etc.

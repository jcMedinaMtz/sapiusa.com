REAL CUSTOMERS REAL STORIES

PROJECT NAME
Grain Weight

OPPORTUNITY
AgTrinsic had a problem while transporting grain from their crop lands to their storage and distribution sites. They would record the weights of each shipment in the origin and in the final destination on paper. This became a problem when large amounts of tickets were to be conciliated with their corresponding payments.

SOLUTION
We developed a mobile app that would be used by the driver so he could input the needed data for each shipment (weight, ticket number, % of humidity, etc.) manually or wireless both at the origin site and final destination.

This allowed the driver to sync the information to his server and later the conciliation would be automatic.

PROJECT NAME
Real Man-Hours

OPPORTUNITY
The man-hours each worker invested on the production line was manually inputted at the end of each shift. This caused uncertainty about the veracity of the data and no data was available until the end of the shift.

SOLUTION
We developed a mobile app to allow supervisors to input the actual man-hours and to take pictures of the status of the products. This information goes directly to a database and it allows the company to have real time information and to have certainty about the veracity of the data.
